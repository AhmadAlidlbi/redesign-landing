Re-Design means a complete redesign from beginning to end. We repair vehicle impacts and restore them back to the original factory design. Our work focuses on repairing and restoring dents and depressions with up to 100% recovery quality where the damage condition allows, using the latest tools, equipment, and techniques.

Our technicians are highly trained to deliver professional repair quality using modern tools and proper techniques. We focus on restoring the factory design, using modern equipment, and following a high-quality repair process from inspection to final finishing.

What is PDR?

PDR stands for Paintless Dent Repair, a technique used to repair suitable dents without repainting while preserving the original factory paint.
